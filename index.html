    Grid tied solar systems are intended for the people who as of now have electric supply help, however wish to lessen their electric bill essentially. With a grid tied solar system , you generate the electricity when the sun rays shine brightly across the sky and produce excessive energy that offers any overabundance back to the electric organization (where relevant). At the point when the Sun isn't sparkling, you simply purchase electrical energy from the grid station, same as everybody else. Solarex uses the monocrystalline solar panels which are considered as the best solar plates among all. These plates give you 20% efficiency in generating the energy. Simply made up of silicon, cylindered shaped and uniform in color. These plates are more durable than any other solar plate for residential usage and give you the maximum warranty up to 30 years depending upon the mono crystalline type. One of the biggest advantages of purchasing grid tied solar panels is that it offers one time investment and the cost of grid tied is very low as compared to the others. Now the Government of Pakistan GOP is providing the facility of Net-Metering which makes the use of solar energy more reliable and convenient for installation.
